Qualtrics Vocalize Dashboard


OVERVIEW

Vocalize Dashboard is a powerful analytic tool for companies to analyze customer data, discover insights, and ultimately better understand customers.


ROLE

  • Led the design from end to end;
  • Worked with UX Researcher and Client Success team on usability research and customer validation.


SCOPE


Qualtrics has been known as a research software company. People use our products to collect data, to capture what customers say about them by doing online NPS/Customer Satisfaction surveys and website intercepts. However, data is only as valuable as the decision it enables. How to turn data into insights, into something actionable? Vocalize Dashboard brings all these data together. It enables users to drill down or up to check specific issues and overall trends, discover insights and solve issues at the strategic level.


DASHBOARD

A dashboard is a set of pages that have all of your customized interactive data visualizations to serve different research and analysis purposes.


SHARE

Vocalize allows users sharing the dashboard with other people in their organization. People who have access are able to adjust the filters to dive into the data that matters to them. The Share modal has the built-in data restrictions and page access settings. In this way, dashboard editors can limit the data that viewers can access.


WIDGETS

We offer more than 25 types of widgets, which all can be customized to present the data in a clean and delighted view. Some of the widgets are interactive meaning users can drill down into charts for more details, interactively change what they see and how it influences the rest of the widgets on the page.


KEY DRIVERS

Key Drivers analyzes the correlations between the customer satisfaction/interest and the potential internal/external factors to see whether there is evidence of a relationship between them. Key Drivers widget enables users to identify the areas of improvement that bring the greatest impact to their customer experience.




RESPONSE TICKER

Response Ticker widget collects all individual survey responses with associated NPS score into one summary. Sometimes words matter more than numbers. This widget allows users to better understand the sentiments behind customer satisfaction numbers.


WHAT’S NEXT

Before users set up a dashboard, they have to first connect their surveys or CSVs to the dashboard and map the data to an empty fieldset. We’ve identified this process as a major barrier for our ease of use goal. The concept is so complex that users already feel not delighted before doing any analysis work. I’m working on a set of solutions to improve the user experience. Contact me if you want to know more.
